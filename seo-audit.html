Prioritized Recommendations

HIGH PRIORITY

1. Write Unique Meta Titles and Descriptions for Every Page

Current: Homepage title is just “Pasture & Petal.” Product titles follow “Name – Pasture & Petal” with no keywords. Meta descriptions are absent.

Fix: Homepage: “Pasture & Petal | 100% Grass-Fed Tallow Skincare — Handmade in Maryland.” Products: “Perennial Glow Tallow Face Moisturizer | Grass-Fed, Vitamin-Rich | Pasture & Petal.” Each page needs a unique 150-160 character meta description.

Impact: Directly improves click-through rates from search results and helps Google understand page topics.

HIGH PRIORITY

2. Fix Product Schema Markup

Current: Product structured data uses “My Store” as brand name (Shopify default).

Fix: Update in Shopify Admin > Settings > General. Change store name to “Pasture & Petal.” This propagates to all Product schema automatically.

Impact: Enables proper brand entity recognition in Google Knowledge Graph and rich snippets.

HIGH PRIORITY

3. Consolidate to Single H1 Per Page

Current: Homepage has three H1 tags: “100% GRASS-FED TALLOW SKINCARE,” “Embrace Simplicity,” and “Why Tallow?”

Fix: Keep only the first as H1. Change others to H2. Edit in Shopify theme code.

Impact: Clarifies primary topic signal for crawlers.

HIGH PRIORITY

4. Set Up Google Business Profile

Current: No GBP despite being a farm-based business at Fiddlers Green Farm, Taneytown, Maryland.

Fix: Claim GBP at business.google.com. Add LocalBusiness schema to website. Upload farm photos. Set business category to “Skin Care Products Store” or “Health & Beauty.”

Impact: Enables Google Maps visibility and local pack rankings with zero competition for “tallow skincare Maryland.”

HIGH PRIORITY

5. Launch Tallow Education Blog

Current: /blogs/news exists in sitemap but contains zero articles.

Fix: Publish 2-4 articles monthly: “Why Tallow Is the Closest Thing to Your Skin’s Natural Oils,” “Tallow vs. Retinol: A Natural Alternative,” “Is Beef Tallow Good for Acne?” “Tallow Skincare During Pregnancy: What to Know.”

Impact: Captures informational search traffic, builds topical authority, creates internal linking opportunities to product pages.

MEDIUM PRIORITY

6. Pursue Roundup Article Inclusions

Current: Not listed on any “best tallow skincare” articles. Competitors appear on The Good Trade, SkinSort, PrettyOrganicGirl, WowMD.

Fix: Pitch to each publication with the farm story, Awesome Foundation grant, and woman-owned angles. Include product samples.

Impact: Each inclusion builds a high-authority backlink and drives referral traffic from purchase-intent readers.

MEDIUM PRIORITY

7. Write Descriptive Alt Text for All Product Images

Current: Generic alt text like “Bare Balm image 3” and “Bare Balm image 4.”

MEDIUM PRIORITY

8. Resolve Duplicate Collection URLs

Current: /collections/all, /collections/all-products, and /category/all-products appear to serve similar content.

Fix: Add canonical tags pointing to the preferred URL. 301 redirect duplicate paths.

Impact: Prevents duplicate content dilution and consolidates ranking signals.

9. Add FAQ Schema Markup to FAQ Page

Current: 16 well-written FAQ questions with no FAQPage structured data.

Impact: FAQ rich results dramatically increase SERP real estate and click-through rates.
